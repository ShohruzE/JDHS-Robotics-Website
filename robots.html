OUR ROBOTS

MAKO

Rapid React 2022

MAKO is our 2022 season challenger that competed in 3 regional events; went 7-6-0 at the New York Tech Valley Regional, 7-7-0 at the SPBLI Long Island Regional #2, and 9-5-1 at the New York City Regional. This season, we were a quarter-finalist at New York Tech Valley Regional, 2-time semi-finalist at SPBLI Long Island Regional #2 and New York City Regional, and the winner of the Creativity Award at New York Tech Valley Regional.

CHICKEN NUGGIE

Infinite Recharge 2020-2021

Our 2020 challenger, CHICKEN NUGGIE unfortunately never played in any regional due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

SOL

Destination: Deep Space 2019

Sol is our 2019 season challenger that competed in 2 regional events and 1 championship event; going for 5-8-1 at New York Tech Valley Regional, 9-5-0 at New York City Regional, and 4-6-0 at Tesla Division at World Championship. We were a quarter-finalist at New York Tech Valley Regional, a semi-finalist at New York City Regional, the winner of the Imagery Award at New York Tech Valley Regional, and Quality Award at New York City Regional.

CLYDE

Power Up 2018

Clyde is our 2018 season challenger, competed in 2 regional and 1 championship events, New York Tech Valley Regional, New York City Regional, and Curie Division. 1-time quarter-finalist, 1-time finalist, championship division winner, and the winner of Imagery Award, Excellence in Engineering Award.

HURRICANE

Steamworks 2017

Hurricane is our 2017 season challenger, competed in 2 regional and 1 championship events, New York Tech Valley Regional, New York City Regional, and Daly Division. 2 times regional finalist, the winner of the New York Tech Valley Regional, Innovation in Control Award, and the Creativity Award.

JESTER

Stronghold 2016

Jester is our 2018 season challenger, competed in 2 regional and 1 championship events, New York Tech Valley Regional, New York City Regional, and Galileo Division. 3-times quarter-finalist, and the winner of Excellence in Engineering Award.

TANG

Recycle Rush 2015

TANG is our 2015 season challenger, competed in 2 regional events, New York Tech Valley Regional, and New York City Regional.

SHRIMPY

Aerial Assist 2014

Shrimpy is our 2014 season challenger, competed in 2 regional and 1 championship events, SBPLI Long Island Regional, and New York City Regional. 1-time semi-finalist, and the winner of Imagery Award.
